25 Questions to Ask on Every Venue Tour

Kitchen & Catering

  1. Can we bring our own Indian caterer with full kitchen access?
  2. What kitchen equipment is on-site? (Gas burners, oven, ventilation, prep tables, refrigeration)
  3. Is there space and power for a tandoor oven (requires 220V or propane)?
  4. What are the kitchen access hours on wedding day and day before?
  5. Is there a separate prep/staging area for caterer setup?

Fire Ceremony

  1. Has a havan/agni ceremony ever been performed here?
  2. What is the specific fire marshal jurisdiction and permit process?
  3. What is the fire safety officer cost for ceremony day?
  4. Is the property in a Cal Fire high or very high severity zone?
  5. What is the indoor backup location if fire ceremony is denied day-of?

Noise & Music

  1. What is the hard-stop time for amplified music? (Need midnight minimum)
  2. Is there an indoor space where music can continue past outdoor cutoff?
  3. Have you ever received a noise complaint? What happened?
  4. Are there decibel limits? Who monitors them?

Logistics & Infrastructure

  1. What is the baraat processional route? (Need 8ft wide, flat, 200ft minimum)
  2. Can a horse be on property? What are the requirements?
  3. Where is the designated haldi area? What surface protection is needed?
  4. What is the mandap ceiling height in the ceremony area? (Need 12ft minimum)
  5. What is the power capacity for lighting, DJ, and catering simultaneously?
  6. Is there ADA accessibility? Golf cart for elderly guests?

Cultural & Multi-Day

  1. Have you hosted an Indian wedding before? Can we contact that couple?
  2. Is multi-day booking available? (Mehndi Fri, Sangeet Fri night, Wedding Sat)
  3. What is the damage deposit and what specifically triggers deductions?
  4. Can we have a late-night after-party space?
  5. What is NOT allowed? (Fireworks, sparklers, open flame, colored powder, animals)
